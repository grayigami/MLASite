What We Do

THE MLA PROGRAM IS AN INNOVATIVE ISLAMIC WEEKEND PROGRAM FOCUSED ON ACADEMIC, SOCIAL, AND MOST IMPORTANTLY SPIRITUAL DEVELOPMENT OF OUR YOUTH. WE OFFER A VARIETY OF CLASSES FROM PRESCHOOL THROUGH 11TH GRADE. OUR TALENTED AND ENTHUSIASTIC STAFFS ARE CERTIFIED TO TEACH IN THEIR RESPECTIVE AREAS OF SPECIALTY, AND HAVE BEEN CAREFULLY SELECTED AND TRAINED BY OUR ADMINISTRATIVE BOARD TO ENSURE THE HIGHEST QUALITY EDUCATION FOR OUR STUDENTS.
